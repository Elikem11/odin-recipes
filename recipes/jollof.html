Jollof

Description

Jollof Rice is a spiced dish, which is simmered in reduced tomatoes onions, peppers and different seosonings depending on where it is made. It is an iconic dish, with massive regional significance across West Africa and a staple in celeberatory social gatherings.

Every West African country has at least one variation of Jollof Rice and it both divides and unites the region. With its sedutive aroma, deep-red colour and spicy flavour, Jollof Rice is the undisputed queen of the West African kitchens. Its preparation methods do vary across different regions.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • fresh tomatoes
  • tinned tomatoes
  • onions
  • vegetable oil
  • pepper
  • chicken
  • vegetables (green pepper, green beans, peas, carrots
  • salt and other spices and seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken to yours specification (fried or grilled)
  2. Blend tomatoes with onion, ginger, garlic, and pepper
  3. Pour oil into cooking pan and heat for about 5mins
  4. Slice some oinions into the hot oil and fry for about 2 minutes
  5. Add your tinned tomatoes to the oil, stir and cook for about 7 minutes
  6. Add blended tomato sauce and let it cook for about 10 minutes/li>
  7. Stir whiles it simmers or boils till the moisture is reduced so you get a gravy
  8. Now wash your rice with clean water and drain itwith a seive
  9. Pour your rice into the gravy sauce
  10. Stir it to make it evenly dispersed
  11. Allow to boil for about 15minutes or until it is dry enough
  12. Chop your vegetables and let it cook for just 4 minutes
  13. Your jollof is ready. Serve with your fried chicken
